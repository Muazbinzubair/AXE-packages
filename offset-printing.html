Offset Printing

Our high-definition offset printing delivers vibrant, full-color printed boxes ideal for product packaging, retail displays, and brand promotion. This premium printing method ensures your products are presented with elegance and clarity, creating a lasting impression on your customers.

Key Features

  • Exceptional print quality with sharp details
  • Vibrant, accurate color reproduction
  • Smooth gradients and fine halftones
  • Ideal for high-end product packaging

Technical Specifications

  • Print Method: Sheet-fed offset lithography
  • Color Capacity: Up to 6-color process plus spot colors
  • Substrate Thickness: 200-400 gsm paperboard
  • Resolution: 300 lpi (lines per inch)
  • Minimum Order: 1,000 pieces
