Corrugated Boxes

Our corrugated boxes provide superior protection for shipping and storage. Available in various flute profiles and board strengths to meet your specific requirements, from lightweight retail packaging to heavy-duty shipping containers.

Key Features

  • Superior protection and cushioning
  • Custom sizes and strengths available
  • Eco-friendly and recyclable materials
  • Cost-effective shipping solutions

Technical Specifications

  • Flute Types: A, B, C, E, F flutes available
  • Board Types: Single, double, and triple wall
  • Burst Strength: Up to 275 lbs/in²
  • Edge Crush Test: Up to 55 lbs/in
  • Print Options: Flexo or litho laminated
